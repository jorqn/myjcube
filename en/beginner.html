Don't be afraid !

Even though it is nearly impossible to solve the rubik's cube as a simple amateur, doing it using a proven technique is something that anyone can do. You will find in this section a very accessible solving procedure. Of course, all formulas will be given in shataro notation, hoping that it will make them easier for you to remember.
